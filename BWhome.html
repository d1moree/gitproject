Experience the best of the Bahamas right here in the Midwest! Located off the southern docks of Bde Maka Ska (formerly Lake Calhoun), and built to model the appearance of old-style Bahamian fishing boats, Bahama Waters offers both traditional Bahamian and American cuisine in a non-traditional atmosphere. Dishes include everything from the American Burger and Fries to Bahamian Baked Macaroni. A favourite of island natives and Minnesotans alike, come and taste the flavours of the Caribbean and Midwestern Homes!
